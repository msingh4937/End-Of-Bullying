Verbal Bullying

What is Verbal Bullying?

Verbal Bullying,is like physical bullying. Infliciting pain. However instead of physical pain, its emotional pain. Its when people are hurt through the usage of words instead of the usage of fists. This type of bullying is probably one of the worst and probably the most terrible type of bulyling.

What is some way people bully using verbal actions?

This type of Bullying is used through words more then physical interaction. Some ways people bully others using this method is, Insulting them using words(like ugly,stupid,or calling them LGBT to insult them) People also insult their friends or family(dead or alive).
